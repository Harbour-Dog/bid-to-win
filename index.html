The Rules

1. Both players get 10 cards numbered 1-10.

2. Once a player has used one of their cards in a bid, it is gone for the rest of the game.

3. Each game consists of five rounds of bidding.

4. A round begins with two randomly generated numbers between 1-10 being placed up for bid. These numbers are called the Tricks.

5. Each player must secretly choose and commit which of their cards they will use to bid for each Trick.

6. Once both players have made their selections, the bids are revealed.

7. The player that bids the highest on a trick, wins that trick and is awarded that many points.

8. The player with the most points after 5 rounds is the winner!
