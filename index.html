The Rules

1. Both players have 10 buttons numbered 1-10.

2. A round begins with two randomly generated numbers between 1-10 being placed up for bid. These numbers are called the Tricks.

3. First, the Blue player must choose their two bids. The first number they click will be for the trick on the left. Red must look away until Blue clicks 'Commit'!

4. Once both players have made their selections, and Red Commits, the bids are revealed.

5. The player that bids the highest on a trick, wins that trick and is awarded that many points.

6. Each game consists of five rounds of bidding.

7. Once a player has used one of their buttons in a bid, it is gone for the rest of the game.

8. The player with the most points after 5 rounds is the winner!

9. The Match Tally keeps track of total wins for each player. Enjoy!
